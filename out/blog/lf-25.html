State of Lo Fi Coffee Address! Here we are one month into 2013! Lo Fi has been doing excellent! We changed out the Nova Simonelli back in December for a one group San Marcos! We are proud to say that our coffee has really seen a drastic improvement! Utilizing the single group machine our time is more focused on pulling quality shots and making sure every drink is uniform for our customers! We have been extremely pleased with the production of the single group!

We are also creating a more stable stock of coffee! Our espresso has never been better and our big 3 selection is great. In the past year we have been able to secure Colombian Huisa Alto De Obispo, Ethiopian Yirgacheffe, and a Guatemalan Antigua that is to die for!

We are working right now on implementing better pastries into our selections. To do this we have tapped the skills of Michelle D. from The Nile, and she has started "Reggie's Vegan Bakery". That's right we now have vegan cupcakes, scones, and cookies on demand at Lo Fi! We also came out with our seasonal flavors after the Holidays! We currently are utilizing Orgeat(or almond) and Irish Cream as our two seasonal flavors!

Why not stop by Lo Fi today and check us out!
